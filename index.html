About Me

As a veteran Pacific Northwest radio personality and voice artist, I will bring a wealth of voiceover, commercials, promo, narration, and imaging experience to your project!

I'm not just another 'announcer,' though! My passion for the craft of voice acting and my dedication to excellence ensure I'll deliver a rich, compelling voice that captivates your audience.

With an expansive technical background complemented by continuous learning in literature, science, and technology, let me communicate your vision - from scripts to stories and beyond - with insightful understanding and a credible tone that captures attention and builds trust with your listeners.

I don't just read words; I convey the emotion and context behind them.
